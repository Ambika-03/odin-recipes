Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian comfort food made by layering pasta sheets with a rich meat sauce, creamy bechamel or ricotta cheese, mozzarella, and Parmeasan cheese. It is baked until golden, bubbly, and full of flavor.

This hearty dish is prefect for family dinners, special occasions, or meal prep because it tastes even better the next day.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500 g ground beef
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • ½ cup Parmesan cheese, shredded
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cook the lassagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. Saute the onion and garlic in olive oil.
  4. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  5. Stir in the tomato sauce and seasonings.
  6. Spread a layer of sauce in a backing dish.
  7. Layer noodles, ricotta, meat sauce, and mozzarella.
  8. Reapeat the layers until all ingredients are used.
  9. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese on top.
  10. Bake for 40-45 minutes.
  11. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving.
