Pasta Recipe

Description

Pasta is a simple and delicious dish that can be prepared with a variety of sauces and incredients. This recipe combines pasta with a flavorful tomato-based sauce, herbs, and cheese for a quick and satisfying meal.

It is perfect for lunch or dinner and can be customized with vegetables of your favorite protein.

Ingredients

  • 250 g pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • ½ cup Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta until al dente.
  2. Drain the pasta and set it aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan.
  4. Saute the garlic until fragrant.
  5. Add the tomato sauce and Italian seasoning.
  6. Simmer for 5-10 minutes.
  7. Mix the cooked pasta into the sauce.
  8. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
  9. Garnish with fresh basil and serve warm.
